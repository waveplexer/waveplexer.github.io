Filter Bank-based Systems

Why use Wavelet Transform?

Filter Bank based solutions have been gaining popularity as these can increase data rates while reducing error rates. The high computational power achieved by onboard processing systems allows these solutions to work efficiently.

Trans-multiplexer systems utilize Cosine and Fourier transformation functions for signal reconstruction and filter design. These however do not provide Perfect Reconstruction of the signals at the output.
